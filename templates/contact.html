ABOUT GCC CLUB

Ghanarampur Cricket Club (GCC) is one of the most passionate and energetic cricket clubs from Ghanarampur, Bandipur, West Medinipur.

Our club regularly organizes multiple sports events, especially cricket tournaments, to encourage young talents and promote sports culture in our area.

The biggest tournament organized by GCC is the GSPL (Ghanarampur Super Premier League), an exciting auction-based cricket tournament featuring talented players, competitive teams, and huge local support.

GCC is not just a cricket club — it is a family of sports lovers working together to create opportunities, unity, discipline, and entertainment through cricket.

We aim to take rural cricket talent to a bigger level and continue building a strong sports community for the future.
